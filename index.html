FAQ

Common questions.

What systems does Tend connect to?

Most common tools — Google Calendar, Outlook, Square, Stripe, QuickBooks, most scheduling software. If you use it, we can probably connect to it. We'll confirm during the audit.

What happens if something breaks?

We monitor Tend 24/7. If something fails, we fix it — usually before you notice. You also get direct support via text or email anytime.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel anytime and we'll help you transition. The $500 setup is non-refundable, but the monthly is pay-as-you-go.

How is this different from Zapier or Make?

Those are tools you build with. Tend is done-for-you. We audit your workflow, build the automations, install them, and keep them running. You don't touch a dashboard.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Tend runs on-premise where possible, meaning your data stays local. For cloud integrations, we use encrypted connections and never store sensitive customer data.

What if I need custom automations later?

Included. As your business changes, we update Tend. New workflow? New integration? Just tell us. It's part of the monthly.
