Sales Playbook

The pitch, the objections, the close. For SMB and Enterprise.

The 60-Second Pitch (SMB)

"You know how you spend hours every week on scheduling, follow-ups, and chasing invoices? We install AI that does all of that automatically. It runs in the background, 24/7. You don't prompt it, you don't open an app — it just runs. Setup is $500, then $200 a month. Takes 48 hours to install. Want to see what it would automate for your business?"

Objection Handling

"I can just use ChatGPT"

"ChatGPT waits for you to ask it something. Tend runs without you. It sends the follow-up at 8am whether you're awake or not. It's the difference between a tool and an employee."

"$200/month is expensive"

"How much do you pay your front desk person? Tend does 20+ hours of their work every month. That's less than $10/hour for a worker that never calls in sick."

"What if it breaks?"

"We monitor it 24/7. If something fails, we fix it — usually before you notice. And you can text us anytime. That's what the $200/month covers."

"I need to think about it"

"Totally get it. While you're thinking — how many follow-up calls are you missing today? How many invoices are sitting unpaid? Every day without Tend is revenue you're leaving on the table."

Discovery Questions

Time Questions

• "How many hours/week do you spend on scheduling?"
• "Who handles follow-ups? How much time does that take?"
• "What's your process for chasing late payments?"

Pain Questions

• "What falls through the cracks most often?"
• "What would you do with 10 extra hours a week?"
• "Have you ever lost a customer because of slow response?"

Money Questions

• "What's your no-show rate?"
• "How much is sitting in unpaid invoices right now?"
• "What does a new customer worth to you over a year?"
